Privacy Policy

Last updated: 07/20/2026

Data Thaw ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to information collected through our services, our website, and our communications with you, including SMS text messaging.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact information such as your name, phone number, email address, and mailing address, provided when you request a quote, book an appointment, become a customer, or otherwise communicate with us.
  • Service-related information such as appointment details, service history, and communication preferences.

We do not purchase, rent, or use third-party contact lists.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested services;
  • Schedule, confirm, and follow up on appointments;
  • Send service-related communications, including by SMS where you have provided your phone number;
  • Improve our services and maintain business records.

SMS/Text Messaging Privacy

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All of the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with, or sold to, any third parties.

We send text messages to two distinct groups, and consent works differently for each.

Business owners who contact us

If you are a business owner and you provide your phone number through the form on our website or in the course of becoming a client, you consent to receive text messages from Data Thaw about your inquiry and your account. Message frequency varies (typically 1–3 messages per inquiry). Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for assistance.

Customers of the businesses we serve

Data Thaw operates an appointment and reactivation messaging program on behalf of local service businesses — always under the client business's own name, and only to that business's existing customers and prior leads. Messages fall into two separately-consented categories: service messages (appointment reminders, scheduling confirmations, and follow-ups on open quotes or service requests) and, for recipients who separately opt in, promotional messages (occasional offers and reactivation messages).

Consent is obtained by the service business at the point the customer provides their mobile number, through affirmative, unchecked-by-default consent controls — a service-message checkbox and a separate, optional marketing checkbox. Marketing consent is never bundled with service consent and is never a condition of purchase. Data Thaw does not purchase, rent, sell, or share contact lists, and does not message anyone the sending business has not obtained consent for. Message frequency varies (typically 1–5 messages per inquiry, appointment, or campaign). Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for assistance.

Our full messaging program details, including the required consent disclosure and standard replies, are documented on our SMS Messaging Program page.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who perform services on our behalf (such as scheduling or messaging platforms), solely as needed to deliver our services;
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process;
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of our business or others.

Data Retention and Security

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information.

Your Choices

  • Text messages: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for help.
  • Email: Use the unsubscribe link in any email or contact us directly.
  • Access or deletion: Contact us using the information below to request access to or deletion of your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age.

Changes to This Policy
