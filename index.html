Questions

Straight answers before we talk.

Is this compliant?
Yes. We only contact people your business already has an existing relationship with — your own leads and past customers, not purchased or scraped lists. Campaigns follow standard opt-out practices, honor unsubscribe/STOP requests immediately, and run under your business name so recipients recognize who's reaching out. If there's contact you're not comfortable with, we simply don't send it.
Do I have to give you my CRM login?
Never. We don't ask for CRM passwords or API keys, and you should never type those into any web form. All we need is the list itself — a CSV/Excel export of your old leads and past customers. On the form above you just tell us which CRM you use; if you'd like help pulling the export, we'll walk you through it on the call.
Where do the leads come from?
From you. These aren't leads we generate or buy — they're the contacts already sitting in your CRM, spreadsheets, or booking software: old quotes, past customers, inquiries that went cold. That's the whole idea. We're mining revenue you've already paid to acquire, not spending on new leads.
What does it cost?
A flat fee based on your product's value per appointment that shows up — and the first 3 are free. That fee is set against what a booked job is actually worth to you, so it stays a small fraction of the revenue each appointment brings in. No retainers, no setup fees, no contracts, no ad spend. You don't pay for no-shows, and you don't pay anything until you've already seen it work.
How fast do results come?
Reactivation campaigns tend to move quickly because these people already know your business — many replies come within the first few days of a campaign going out. The exact pace depends on the size and age of your list, but you'll typically see booked appointments in the first week or two, not months down the road.
